## About Us
We are a team of passionate thinkers and builders committed to enabling more people and institutions to gain exposure to Decentralised Finance and crypto. Our mission is to provide the most trusted entry point to DeFi. Our [Oasis.app](https://oasis.app/) products give the best user experience in the industry and inspire the highest confidence levels.

We are a remote-first business, with most of our team spread across Europe and a few of us in the Americas and Asia. We operate under Oazo Apps Limited, a company incorporated and registered in the United Kingdom.

[Oasis.app](https://oasis.app/) is going through an exciting growth stage, having recently raised a $6m Series A round, and we have ambitious growth plans for 2022 and beyond.

## The role
Our Marketing Team is currently a full-time Marketing Lead and a part-time marketing and research specialist. We are looking for a DeFi-savvy professional to join us as a Community Manager part-time. Your primary responsibility will be building and maintaining [Oasis.app](https://oasis.app/) community and public perception. You will help manage communications with our community and handle responsibility for the growth of our main communication channels (esp. Twitter and Discord).

We’re looking for people experienced in social media, focusing on Web3 and DeFi. If you are a self-driven and ambitious professional, we would love to meet you! 

## Community Manager responsibilities
- Build, maintain and ensure the growth of [Oasis.app](https://oasis.app/) community and public perception
- Set, plan and implement social media campaigns and strategies

- Host community calls and events
- Provide engaging text, image and video content for all social media
- Monitor, track and report on feedback and online reviews
- Stay up-to-date with digital technology trends
- Coordinate with the Marketing Lead
- Collaborate closely with the Product team
